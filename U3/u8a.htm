Unit 8

In Unit 8, I learned how to create mobile apps using the MIT App Inventor platform. Through this unit, I gained hands-on experience in building apps from scratch, incorporating various interactive components. One highlight was developing my own app specifically designed for 7-year-old pupils to assist them in learning the alphabet. The app featured engaging activities, games, and exercises to make learning the alphabet an enjoyable experience. This project allowed me to combine my app development skills with a focus on creating educational tools tailored for young learners. Overall, Unit 8 provided me with a solid foundation in mobile app development and the opportunity to create a practical and meaningful application for early literacy education.
