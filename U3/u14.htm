Unit 14

In Unit 14, I gained valuable knowledge and practical skills related to installing and maintaining computer hardware components. This unit covered the essential steps and best practices for properly setting up and configuring various hardware components, including motherboards, processors, memory modules, storage devices, and peripheral devices. Throughout the unit, I learned how to carefully handle and install these components while adhering to manufacturer guidelines and safety protocols. Additionally, I acquired troubleshooting techniques to identify and resolve common hardware issues that may arise during the installation or maintenance process. By successfully completing Unit 14, I demonstrated my proficiency in hardware installation and maintenance, ensuring that computer systems are properly assembled, configured, and maintained for optimal performance and reliability.
