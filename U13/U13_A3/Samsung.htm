The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra is a powerhouse of a phone, featuring a 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED display and a Qualcomm SM8550-AC Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 (4 nm) chipset. It runs on Android 13, upgradable to Android 14, with One UI 6. The phone boasts a stunning 200 MP camera with OIS and PDAF, and is available with 8GB or 12GB of RAM and storage options of 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB. The device is powered by a 5000 mAh battery with 45W wired and 15W wireless charging capabilities. It comes in a range of colors including Phantom Black, Green, Cream, Lavender, Graphite, Sky Blue, Lime, Red, and BMW M Edition. The Galaxy S23 Ultra also features Samsung DeX, Ultra Wideband (UWB) support, and is rated IP68 for water and dust resistance .
