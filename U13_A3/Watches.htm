The Apple Watch Ultra 2 comes with a range of impressive specifications, making it a top choice for smartwatch enthusiasts. Here are the key specs:

Dimensions: 49mm case, 44mm width, 14.4mm depth, and a weight of 61.4g.

Design: Aerospace-grade titanium case, dual speakers, and a three-microphone array with beamforming and wind noise mitigation.

Display: Always-On Retina LTPO OLED display with a peak brightness of up to 3000 nits and a flat sapphire crystal display.

Chip: S9 SiP with a 64-bit dual-core processor, 4-core Apple Neural Engine, and 64GB capacity.

Sensors: Blood oxygen sensor, electrical heart sensor, ECG app, third-generation optical heart sensor, temperature sensing, and more.

Connectivity: Precision dual-frequency GPS, LTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, second-generation Ultra Wideband chip, and support for Apple Pay and GymKit.

Power and Battery Life: Built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery, providing up to 36 hours of normal use and up to 72 hours in Low Power Mode.

It comes with a USB-C magnetic fast-charging cable.

Environmental Requirements: The watch is designed to operate within a wide range of temperatures and environmental conditions, including water resistance for swimming and diving.

In the Box: The package includes the Apple Watch Ultra 2, a band/loop, and an Apple Watch Magnetic Fast Charger to USB-C Cable (1m).

These specifications highlight the advanced technology, robust design, and versatile functionality of the Apple Watch Ultra 2, making it a standout choice in the smartwatch market.

The Samsung Galaxy Watch 6 comes with a range of impressive specifications, making it a top choice for smartwatch enthusiasts. Here are the key specs:

Versions: Available in 44mm and 40mm versions, with options for Bluetooth only or LTE connectivity.

Display: Super AMOLED display with a size of 1.5 inches, a resolution of 480 x 480 pixels, and a pixel density of approximately 453 ppi. The display is protected by Sapphire crystal and features an always-on display.

Dimensions: For the 44mm version, the dimensions are 42.8 x 44.4 x 9 mm, with a weight of 33.3g. For the 40mm version, the dimensions are slightly smaller, and the weight is 28.7g.

Build: The watch features a glass front (Sapphire crystal) and an aluminum frame. It is MIL-STD-810H compliant and 50m water-resistant (IP68). It also has an ECG certified and blood pressure monitor.

Platform: The watch runs on Android Wear OS 4 and One UI Watch 5, powered by the Exynos W930 (5 nm) chipset and a dual-core 1.4GHz Cortex-A55 CPU.

Memory: It comes with 16GB of internal storage and 2GB of RAM.

Connectivity: The watch supports Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.3, A2DP, LE, GPS, GLONASS, GALILEO, BDS, and NFC. It does not have a 3.5mm jack or a USB port.

Sensors: The watch features an accelerometer, gyro, compass, heart rate sensor, barometer, and thermometer for skin temperature.

Battery: It is powered by a non-removable Li-Ion 425mAh battery, with 10W wireless (Qi) charging support.

Price: The price for the Samsung Galaxy Watch 6 is £319.00 / €295.00.

These specifications highlight the advanced technology, robust design, and versatile functionality of the Samsung Galaxy Watch 6, making it a standout choice in the smartwatch market.
